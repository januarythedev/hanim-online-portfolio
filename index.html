about

I'm Hanim, an aspiring front-end developer based in Johor Bahru, Malaysia who has a deep passion for website design & development. I find joy in crafting seamless and visually appealing digital experiences that stays true to one’s own brand. Constantly learning and adapting, I aspire to contribute positively to the ever-evolving world of web development.
